# Harvest Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 14, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Account Data:** Email, display name, and avatar when you sign up.
- **Location Data:** We collect your precise location when using the map and posting listings to show them to nearby neighbors.
- **Listings & Messages:** Any photos, descriptions, and chats you post.

## 2. How We Use Information
- To facilitate the hyperlocal sharing of surplus food.
- Exact pickup locations are only shared with the specific user who reserves your listing.

## 3. Data Deletion
You may delete your account at any time from the Profile tab. This will delete all your active listings and personal data.

*For closed beta only.*
